About me

civilization does not advance automatically. my work focuses on understanding the forces that drive civilizational progress, and on researching and building meta-technologies that sustain and accelerate it. alongside this broad mission, i remain committed to the human scale: art, design, music, sport, and engineering as essential forms of human expression. these practices ground my everyday life and inform my professional work.

my specialty lies in assembling interdisciplinary teams of artists, engineers, designers, and other contributors, aligning them into functional groups that communicate across disciplines and produce diverse work.

i divide my time between two complementary tracks:

  • euclidean studios — indie game development, where i co-lead nazralath: the fallen world.
  • international committee of the red cross (icrc) — virtual reality simulation work, building training experiences for high-stakes humanitarian contexts, and supporting recruitment through interviews and local community outreach to grow an interdisciplinary team.

i also served on the board of the serbian games association (sga) and participate in lectures, workshops, and strategy work for the regional games ecosystem.
