About me

Civilization doesn't advance on its own. I'm trying to deeply understand what causes civilizational progress, as well as research and build meta-technologies that would help with maintaining and accelerating our advancement.

On a less grand scheme, I'm in love with Humanity and the things we make, do and consume, so to stay sane and maintain a sense of normalcy in everyday life, I play with art, music, sports, dances and engineering.
